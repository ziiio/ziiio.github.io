About

Ziiio builds turn-by-turn navigation systems for large-scale venues. We specialize in making it easy for your visitors to navigate hundreds of locations and millions of square feet without getting lost. We provide a personal intelligent guide for each of your visitors to find any event, person, or location in your facility.

We believe in simplicity. We integrate what we build with your building management, calendaring, directory, and other services to provide seamless management.
